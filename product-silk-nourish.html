How to Use

Apply a sufficient amount of oil to your scalp and hair. Massage gently using your fingertips for 5–10 minutes. Leave it on for at least 1–2 hours or overnight for best results. Wash with a mild shampoo. Use 2–3 times a week or as needed.

Caution

  • For external use only.
  • Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Discontinue use if irritation occurs.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
