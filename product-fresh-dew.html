How to Use

Apply a small amount of body wash onto wet skin or a sponge. Massage gently to create a rich lather, then rinse thoroughly with clean water. Use daily for a refreshing and comfortable bathing experience.

Caution

  • For external use only.
  • Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.
  • Discontinue use if irritation develops.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
