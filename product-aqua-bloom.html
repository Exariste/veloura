How to Use

Apply an appropriate amount onto wet skin or a bath sponge. Massage gently to create a rich lather, then rinse thoroughly with clean water. Use daily for a refreshing and luxurious shower experience.

Caution

  • For external use only.
  • Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse immediately with clean water.
  • If irritation develops, discontinue use.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
