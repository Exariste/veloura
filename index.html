Our Story

A brand born from nature, built for radiant living.

Veloura began with a simple belief: personal care should feel like a ritual, not a routine. Every formula is developed around ingredients that have nourished skin and hair for generations — reimagined with modern, dermatologically inspired care and packaged for the world stage.

Veloura Personal Care is a proud subsidiary brand of Exariste Group, a diversified conglomerate committed to innovation, quality, sustainability, and customer excellence across industries.
