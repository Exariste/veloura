How to Use

Apply an adequate amount to wet hair and scalp. Massage gently to create a rich lather, then rinse thoroughly with clean water. Repeat if necessary. For best results, use regularly as part of your hair care routine.

Caution

  • For external use only.
  • Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse immediately with clean water.
  • If irritation develops, discontinue use.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
